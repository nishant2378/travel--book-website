around the world

Nestled in the lap of the majestic Himalayas, Uttarakhand is one of India’s most serene and spiritually rich states often called the “Devbhumi”, or Land of the Gods. A true jewel in the crown of northern India, Uttarakhand enchants travelers from around the world with its blend of natural beauty, ancient heritage, thrilling adventure, and divine peace.

fastest travel

The quickest way to reach Uttarakhand is by flight. The state’s main airport, Jolly Grant Airport in Dehradun, offers direct connections from Delhi, Mumbai, Bangalore, and other major cities. From here, you can quickly access Rishikesh, Haridwar, Mussoorie, and nearby hill stations by road within 1–3 hours.
